# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in the Meten community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience or ability.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive and empathetic.
- Welcome questions and differing viewpoints.
- Give and accept actionable feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for users and the community.
- Respect privacy and avoid sharing others' personal information.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized conduct, personal attacks and sustained disruption are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to `conduct@meten.com`. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior violates this policy. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical.

This policy is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
