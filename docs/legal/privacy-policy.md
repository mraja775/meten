# Meten Privacy Policy

Effective: July 20, 2026

Meten provides academy management and student training tools. This policy describes the information processed by the Meten customer Android application and its supporting service.

## Information we process

- Account identifiers, including student name and email address supplied by the academy.
- Score-sheet photographs uploaded by the student.
- Training dates, notes, reflections, OCR text, extracted scores, corrections, and derived performance summaries.
- Security and operational information such as session identifiers, request timestamps, device/network metadata made available to the service, and error logs.

## Why we process it

We use this information to authenticate students, store and display their training history, extract score information, provide progress summaries, secure the service, troubleshoot failures, and support the student's academy. We do not sell personal information or use customer training data for third-party advertising.

## Sharing and storage

Information is available to the authenticated student and authorized personnel of their academy. Service providers may process limited information for hosting, object storage, email delivery, security, and monitoring under contractual safeguards. Data may be processed where those providers operate and is protected using access controls and encryption in transit.

## Retention and deletion

We retain records while the academy account is active and as needed for legitimate operational, contractual, legal, backup, and security purposes. Students may request access, correction, or deletion through their academy or by emailing privacy@meten.com. Some records may be retained where legally required or preserved temporarily in backups.

## Security and children

We use short-lived one-time codes, revocable opaque sessions, tenant-scoped authorization, HTTPS, and restricted storage credentials. No system is perfectly secure. Academies are responsible for obtaining any consent required for minors and for providing appropriate guardian notices.

## Contact and changes

Questions and privacy requests may be sent to privacy@meten.com. We may update this policy as the service changes and will publish a revised effective date. Deployers must publish this policy at a stable public HTTPS URL and update the contact details and jurisdiction-specific disclosures before store submission.
